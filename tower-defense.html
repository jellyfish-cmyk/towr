How to Play:

  • Click on a tower type, then click on the path to place it
  • Towers can only be placed on the enemy path
  • Towers automatically attack nearby enemies
  • Prevent enemies from reaching the end of the path
  • Earn money by defeating enemies
  • Buy better towers to survive stronger waves
  • When the layout changes, towers not on the new path go to inventory
  • Click on inventory towers to place them back on the path
  • Watch out! Enemies can destroy your towers!
