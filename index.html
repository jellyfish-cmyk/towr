Game Features

4 Unique Towers

Basic, Sniper, Splash, and Freeze towers each with unique abilities and strategies.

4 Enemy Types

Face basic, fast, tank, and boss enemies with different health and speed characteristics.

Wave System

Progressive difficulty with increasingly challenging waves and stronger enemies.

Resource Management

Earn money by defeating enemies and spend wisely on strategic tower placement.

Statistics Tracking

Monitor tower performance with detailed kill counts and damage statistics.

Responsive Design

Play seamlessly on desktop, tablet, or mobile devices with optimized controls.

How to Play

1

Choose Your Tower

Select from 4 different tower types, each with unique abilities and costs.

2

Place on Path

Click on the enemy path to place towers. Green preview means valid placement.

3

Start the Wave

Click "Start Wave" to begin enemy spawning and watch your towers defend.

4

Survive & Upgrade

Earn money, buy better towers, and survive as many waves as possible!
